About St. Peter's School

St. Peter's School (SPS) is a reputed institution in Purnea, Bihar. It emphasizes academic excellence along with holistic development.

Mission & Vision

Foster curiosity, creativity, and compassion in learners through modern education and moral values.

Programs & Activities

  • Primary and secondary education with English-medium curriculum.
  • Computer lab, smart classrooms, project-based learning.
  • Sports, arts, cultural clubs, karate, and co-curricular events.

Facilities

Well-equipped campus with playgrounds, laboratories, and indoor games.
